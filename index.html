Hello there!

My name is Adam and it gives me great pleasure to welcome you to my new website. Originally from Carrickfergus, Northern Ireland, I relocated to Wadsworth, Ohio. I am a senior at the University of Akron majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics. My aspiration is to be a professional web developer when I graduate in May 2020. The reason for this is because I love combining my creativity with my problem solving nature. Ever since I was a child I loved computers and I was fascinated by them. I would make very basic websites using site builders, however, they are very limited in what you can do with them. I realized it was time for me to take it to the next level. In order for me to develop my web development skills I have produced this website.

I hope you take a look around and check out some of my other work in the "Experience" tab above, and don't be afraid to leave me some feedback in the contact section.

Adam
